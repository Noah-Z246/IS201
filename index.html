About Me

I’m currently a pre-business major at BYU in Provo, driven by a deep desire to learn, grow, and make a meaningful impact. I recently returned from serving a religious mission in Upstate New York, where I developed a love for God and for the people I served. That experience strengthened my communication skills, deepened my empathy, and taught me the importance of service—qualities I aim to carry into both my future career and family life. I'm passionate about personal growth, meaningful connections, and building a life centered on purpose and contribution.

Since beginning my college education, I’ve developed a strong interest in various aspects of business, especially marketing, business management, and entrepreneurship. I’ve gained hands-on experience in building and promoting business ideas and have grown confident working with tools in information systems. My technical skills include VBA, SQL, HTML, and database organization and analysis. These skills are helping me build a solid foundation for my future in business and technology.

Outside of academics and professional interests, I’m someone who values connection, creativity, and meaningful experiences. In my free time, I enjoy fishing, playing pickleball, watching movies, and both attending and participating in musical theater productions. I also deeply value quality time with family and friends and believe in building relationships that last.

Above all, I strive to love and serve God in everything I do. My goal is to reflect His light through my words, actions, and the way I treat others—whether in the classroom, the workplace, or at home. I believe that true success comes not just from achievement, but from doing good in His sight and building a life centered on faith, service, and integrity.
